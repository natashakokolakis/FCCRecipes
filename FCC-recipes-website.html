CHILI

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbs chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1½ tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 large can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 4 cups of your preferred veggies
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 package of veggie ground
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tbs cilantro
  • Garnishes: avocado, sour creme, cheese, etc.

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse canned chickpeas and beans.
  2. Chop veggies into pieces no larger than 2x2".
  3. Dump all ingredients into an instant pot and mix, adding water as needed.
  4. Set to "chili" setting and wait.
  5. Serve with garnishes.

MAPLE "BACON"

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of tofu or tempeh
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbs maple syrup
  • 3 tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ cup constarch

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients into a large bowl, not including the constarch and tofu/tempeh.
  3. If using tofu, cut into small cubes, or separate tempeh strips then toss into the bowl and mix until covered.
  4. Add cornstarch and mix until there is no visible powder.
  5. Dump onto a baking sheet and bake for 45 minutes.

*Note: if using tofu you must drain, wrap with some paper towel, and crush under some books for at least an hour before starting this recipe

**Allowing the tofu/tempeh to marinade for at least 2 hours before baking is preferred

OVERLOADED SALAD

Ingredients

  • 1 large bag of spinach
  • 1 package of feta
  • 300 g walnuts crushed
  • 20 small beets
  • 3 tbs olive oil
  • ½ tbs oregano
  • bottle of balsamic vinegar
  • Maple Bacon yield
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Rinse and peel beets before cutting into bite sized pieces.
  3. Drizzle beets in olive oil and mix in oregano, salt, and pepper
  4. Spread onto a large baking sheet and bake for about 60 minutes
  5. Toss spinach into a bowl, then add desired quantity of the remaining ingredients.
